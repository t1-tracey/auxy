Welcome to the Auxy sound wiki, a quick easy-to-use reference guide for the sounds, their controls, and prominent tracks that utilize the sounds.

Any unique behavior of macros such as delay and reverb will be noted, otherwise most instruments act very similar with these macros and functionality is (mostly) the same.
